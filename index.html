Press start to begin the game

How To Play

  • Each player is given 4 cards
  • At the start of each turn you are dealt a card with a value of 1-10
  • The person to reach 20 or as close to 20 wins the set
  • You can choose to play a card from your hand
  • When you are done with your turn, press end turn
  • If you are happy with your score, you can stand. Standing will pause all cards being dealt to your board
  • When both players are standing, the scores are evaluated and the winner is the closes to 20 without going over 20
